How to Play

Face your opponent. Count together: “Rock, Paper, Scissors, Shoot!” — on “Shoot,” both players reveal their shape. Determine the winner based on the rules below.

Rock ✊, Paper 🤚, Scissors ✌️ — a fast-paced hand game of reflex and strategy. Each player chooses one of three shapes and reveals it at the same time. The winner is decided instantly based on simple rules.

Moves & Matchups

✊
ROCK
🤚
PAPER
✌️
SCISSORS
  • ✊ Rock crushes ✌️ Scissors → Rock wins
  • ✌️ Scissors cut 🤚 Paper → Scissors win
  • 🤚 Paper covers ✊ Rock → Paper wins
  • If both players choose the same shape → It’s a tie
